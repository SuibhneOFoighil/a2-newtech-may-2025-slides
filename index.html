Molus at a Glance

Molus runs shared infrastructure for micro app builders

Our platform provides hosting, branching, and a userbase for app builders

So builders can hook in and make something wonderful

User Experience

Molus maintains a portfolio of apps. Most apps are free. No login required.

Some apps need to be configured to use external resources

Users sign up to bring their own external resources

User Feedback & Analytics

Users can provide feedback on the apps

Molus tracks low-level engagement metrics

Molus builds a behavioral model, exposing it to builders to enhance the user experience

Revenue Model

Molus offers a subscription service for apps needing external resources

Earnings are distributed to builders of the most-used apps

Molus takes a small cut of each earning to cover its costs
